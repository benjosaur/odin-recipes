Ramen

Description

This recipe is for a ramen bowl on a rainy day.

Ingredients

  • Instant Noodles
  • Eggs
  • (Optional) Herbs
  • (Optional) Cheese

Steps

  1. Put 500ml of water in a pan, and boil to simmer
  2. Crack the eggs and put in boiling water
  3. Add ramen instant noodles and seasoning powder and simmer for 5m
  4. Serve with optional herbs and cheese
